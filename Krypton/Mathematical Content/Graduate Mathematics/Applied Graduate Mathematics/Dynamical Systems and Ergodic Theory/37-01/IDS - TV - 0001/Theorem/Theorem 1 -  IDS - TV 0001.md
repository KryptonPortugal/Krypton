Let $R_{\alpha}$ denote circle rotation.
Then we have that:
- $\alpha \in \mathbb{Q}$ we have that all **orbits** $\sigma_{x}$ are periodic
- $\alpha \in \mathbb{R} \setminus \mathbb{Q}$ we have that all **orbits** $\sigma_{x}$ are dense
